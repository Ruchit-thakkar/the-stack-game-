To use custom MP3 sound effects instead of the default real-time synthesized audio:

1. Place your own custom MP3 files inside this folder:
   - drop.mp3 (Block placement sound)
   - perfect.mp3 (First perfect placement sound)
   - combo.mp3 (Consecutive perfect placements sound)
   - gameover.mp3 (Failure sound)
   - newrecord.mp3 (High score celebration sound)
   - click.mp3 (UI button clicks)

2. Once you place the files here, the game can load and play them directly.
